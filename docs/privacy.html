Privacy Policy

For the Revela app · Last updated 21 July 2026

This policy explains what information the Revela app (“Revela,” “we,” “us”) collects, why, and what choices you have. Revela is an AI-assisted video editor for short-form creators. It is operated by Daniel Tadesse. If you have questions, contact us at daniel.t7504@gmail.com.

The short version

  • To edit a video, Revela uploads a compressed copy of your footage to Google’s Gemini API for analysis. This is core to how the app works.
  • You sign in with Sign in with Apple. We store a Supabase account tied to your Apple identifier so we can track your usage against your plan.
  • We do not sell your data or use your footage to train our own models.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data from inside the app at any time.

Information we collect

  • Account identity. When you use Sign in with Apple, we receive an Apple user identifier and, on first sign-in, the name and email you choose to share (your email may be an Apple private-relay address). We create a corresponding user record in our backend.
  • Your video and audio content. The footage you choose to edit, and audio you record for voiceover. A compressed proxy of your footage is uploaded for AI analysis (see “How your footage is processed”). Speech in your video is transcribed on your device to align edits to word boundaries.
  • Usage and cost data. We record how many edits and style analyses you run and the AI token counts they consume, to enforce plan limits and prevent abuse.
  • Device notification token. If you enable notifications, we store an Apple Push Notification token so we can tell you when a long analysis or export finishes.
  • Diagnostics. Basic technical logs needed to operate and debug the service.

Revela does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

How your footage is processed

When you ask Revela to analyze or edit a video, a compressed proxy copy is uploaded to Google’s Gemini API, which returns a structured “edit plan” describing suggested cuts. The final, full-resolution video is assembled on your device and saved to your camera roll only when you choose to export. Voice-isolation for voiceover audio is processed by ElevenLabs. These providers act as our processors for the purpose of delivering the feature you requested; we do not authorize them to use your content for their own purposes.

Who we share information with

We share information only with service providers that help us run the app:

  • Google (Gemini API) — AI analysis of your uploaded video proxy.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, and push notification delivery.
  • Supabase — our backend: authentication, database, and server functions.
  • ElevenLabs — voice isolation for recorded voiceover.

We do not sell personal information.

Retention

Uploaded video proxies are transient and are used to produce your edit plan; analysis job records are automatically cleaned up shortly after they complete. Your account record and usage counts are retained while your account exists. When you delete your account, we delete your account record, your job records, and your usage data.

Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account. Use the “Delete account” option in the app’s profile screen. This removes your account and associated data from our backend.
  • Permissions. Photo library, microphone, and speech-recognition access are requested only for the features that need them, and can be changed in iOS Settings.
  • Access or questions. Email daniel.t7504@gmail.com and we will respond as required by applicable law such as GDPR/CCPA where relevant.

Children

Revela is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new “last updated” date.
